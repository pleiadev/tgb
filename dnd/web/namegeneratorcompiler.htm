Designed to be useful for DMs working on adventures or encounters for 3rd Edition Dungeons and Dragons game from Wizards of the Coast. Feel free to use this in your campaign and pass it along to your friends. Hopefully, it will add a little flair to your dungeon that you just didn't have time to spend detailing.

This utility compiles a name source for the random name generator. Use it to create customized lists of random names sources for use in your campaign. It is inspired by Chris Pound's name generator at http://www.ruf.rice.edu/~pound who did the real work of coming up with the algorithms. I highly suggest you visit his site to obtain a dataset to use with this file. If you have a particularly interesting modification or just plain old comments send it to me via email.
