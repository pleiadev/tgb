New Monsters

Beware of the modules and source material that come to be used in a campaign. New material can be unbalanced or of poor quality. If you want a new monster take an existing creature and make minor changes like changing a resistance type or appearance but leave all the statistics the same. Adding templates and class levels make for unusual monsters.  Want to copy a monster manual entry? Try this utility to copy-paste Monster Manual entries in compressed stat block format.
